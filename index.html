About Salon Poke

A Bristol lounge built around
the Japanese Pokemon TCG

Salon Poke is where Bristol collectors come to open boxes, share pulls and chase the chase. We curate only factory-sealed Japanese booster boxes — the real deal, shrink-wrapped, straight from Tokyo.

Six nights a week we run a themed Pack Opening Night with limited seats. We hold new-release pre-orders, run a loyalty stamp card, host private group nights and trade singles at the counter. Bring a friend. Bring a box. Bring your chase list.

Privacy Notice (GDPR-compliant)

Who we are: Salon Poke Ltd (Company No. 14857291), 60A Park Row, Bristol BS1 5LE. We're the "data controller" for any personal info you give us.

What we collect: name, email, phone (optional), booking details, purchase history, loyalty stamp count. We don't collect payment card details — those are handled by our PCI-compliant processor SumUp.

How we use it: to process your booking, contact you about reservations and events, send you the weekly newsletter (only if you opt in), and run our loyalty programme. We never sell your data.

How long we keep it: booking & purchase records for 6 years (HMRC requirement). Marketing list until you unsubscribe. Loyalty stamp records for 24 months of inactivity.

Your rights: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection. Email privacy@salonpoke.co.uk — we respond within 30 days.

Complaints: ICO, Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow SK9 5AF.

Terms of Sale

Pricing: All prices include VAT at 20%. Prices may change without notice; the price displayed at the time of order is the price you pay.

Sealed products: Refundable within 14 days as long as shrink-wrap is intact and the JP distributor sticker is unbroken. Once opened, all sales are final.

Singles & accessories: 14-day return for unopened / unused items. Graded cards are non-returnable once removed from the case.

Pre-orders: £20 deposit secures your slot. Balance due on collection day. Full refund if cancelled 7+ days before release; 50% refund inside 7 days; no refund after release day.

Pack Opening Nights: £8 single / £24 bundle / sealed box price. Seats held until 15 min after start; released after that. By booking you accept our House Rules.

Trade-Ins: Quotes valid for 7 days. Cash payouts over £1,000 paid by bank transfer only (AML regulations).

Governing law: England & Wales. Disputes go to small claims court at Bristol Combined Court.
